Spinach and Cannelloni

Ingredients

Sauce:

  • 2tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 Onion Finely Chopped
  • 4 Garlic Cloves Minced
  • 1/2 tsp each dried thyme and oregano
  • 1/3 cup tomato paste
  • 800g canned crushed tomato
  • 4 cups vegetable stock
  • 1/3 cup Chardonnay
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1/3 sp black pepper
  • 2/3 cup basil leaves

Filling:

  • 250g frozen chopped spinach
  • 500g ricotta
  • 1/3 cup parmesan
  • 1 cup shredded cheese Mozzarella preferred
  • 1 egg
  • 1 large garlic clove
  • 3/4 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

Cannelloni

  • 18-22 cannelloni tubes (250g Pack)
  • 1/3 cup parmesan

Recipe

Sauce:

  1. Saute - Heat oil in a small pot over medium heat. Add garlic, onion, thyme and oregano

    Cook for 3-4 minutes until onion is translucent add tomato paste and cook for 1 minute

    2. Reduce wine - Add wine, increase heat to high and let it simmer rapidly until mostly evaporated

  2. Blitz - Remove bay leaf then blitz with a stick blender until smooth.

    Simmer for 1 more minute then remove from the stove.

  3. Basil - Stir in basil then cover to keep warm until required

Filling:

  1. Squeeze Spinach - Place spinach in a colander and press out most of the liquid
  2. Mix Filling - Place spinach in bowl with remaining filling ingredients.
